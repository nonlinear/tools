    Can I do an e-book holdings comparison?

    As of December 2011, Portico does not yet offer an e-book holdings comparison. However, we are actively developing one and would be happy to speak with librarians further about this.

    How long do they take?

    E-journal holdings comparisons can take anywhere from 2 days to 2 weeks. If you have a time commitment, please let us know at the time you request a holdings comparison.

    Why would I want to run a holdings comparison?

    We understand librarians use holdings comparisons primarily for three purposes:

    1. when making choices about deaccessioning print holdings,
    2. when making purchasing selections about moving to e-only licenses, and
    3. when considering joining Portico.
