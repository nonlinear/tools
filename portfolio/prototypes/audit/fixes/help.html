What may auditors do with content in the audit interface?

Per the Portico Terms of Use , "Access to the Content shall be available to Providers and designated staff at each Participating Institution and Qualified Institution via password-controlled access only for verification and audit or testing purposes. This auditing privilege is not intended to be used for delivering Content to patrons, or as a replacement for commercial documents delivery services or to fulfill Interlibrary Loan."
