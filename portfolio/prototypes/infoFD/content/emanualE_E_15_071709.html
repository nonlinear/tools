Many FreshDirect employees have family outside the New York City area – either elsewhere in the United States or in a foreign country – and many of these employees routinely express interest in making extended trips to visit their families. In order to accommodate this interest, FreshDirect has a summer travel leave program. Under the program, employees may apply for an available summer travel leave slot. To be eligible, an employee must have (a) at least 6 months of continuous service with the Company as of the date the leave is to begin, (b) fewer than 3 unscheduled absences in the 6 months leading up to his or her request, and (c) no discipline instances in the 6 months leading up to his or her request. (Subsequent absenteeism or disciplinary issues may result in revocation of a previously granted summer travel leave.) Application forms are available from Human Resources or directly from department managers. Leave under the program must be at least 3 weeks and no longer than 8 weeks and must start and finish between July 1 and August 31 only.

Summer travel leave is without pay (although employees with accrued PTO may elect to use PTO for any part of the leave). Employees, however, will be eligible for $65/week of summer travel leave pay, subject to normal withholdings. Summer travel leave pay will be paid for non-PTO time to employees in the program who: (a) begin their leave and return to work on the dates agreed to with their supervisors and (b) upon returning to work after summer travel leave work for at least one full week. An employee who fails to return as scheduled or who returns to work but is terminated or terminates his or her employment before working a full week post-leave will not be entitled to any summer travel leave pay.

Each department manager will have the discretion to deviate from these general guidelines based on department needs and special circumstances.
