If you were an employee of FreshDirect with at least twelve (12) months of continuous employment, and were rehired within six (6) months of your termination date, you will be eligible to continue your benefits and pay rate (if hourly and if returning to the same job) at the level you enjoyed at the time of your termination of previous employment with FreshDirect and you will retain your original date of hire for purposes of benefits entitlement where length of service is a factor.
