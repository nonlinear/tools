Make certain you are aware of and utilize all FreshDirect methods of communication, including this Employee Manual, bulletin boards, discussions with your immediate supervisor, company memoranda, staff meetings, newsletters, training sessions, and company e-mail and intranet.

You will receive other information booklets, such as your insurance booklets, from time to time. You may take these booklets home so that your family may know more about your job and your benefits.

Bulletin Board

Announcements of general and special interest, as well as important matters affecting employment will be posted on physical and electronic bulletin boards. This will include information on safety and policy changes. The Company urges employees to check the notices on these boards regularly. Employees are not permitted to post any notices on the bulletin board.
