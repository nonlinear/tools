The Company makes a constant effort to be sure that all employees are treated fairly and considerately. However, in any large group, disagreements or misunderstandings may arise which may not be satisfactorily resolved. We would like to be aware of problems when they first arise, so that they may be resolved at the earliest possible stage. The Company maintains an open door policy. Our Open Door Policy means you can feel free to discuss any problem or suggestion with any one in management. You may also discuss any problem or area of concern with, or ask any question to, your immediate supervisor

The “Open Door” is not intended to bypass your immediate supervisor. However, if an employee feels uncomfortable approaching his or her supervisor, the employee is not required to do so and should feel free to contact another manager or Human Resources.
