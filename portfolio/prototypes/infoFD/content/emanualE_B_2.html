Upon accepting employment with FreshDirect, you were or will be asked to sign a Confidentiality Agreement, which generally provides that you will not disclose or use any FreshDirect confidential information, either during or after your employment. Your employment with FreshDirect assumes an obligation to maintain confidentiality, even after you stop working at FreshDirect. Depending on your position at FreshDirect, you may also be asked to sign a Non-Competition Agreement.

Additionally, our customers and suppliers entrust FreshDirect with important information relating to their businesses or personal information. The nature of this relationship requires maintenance of confidentiality. In safeguarding the information received, FreshDirect earns the respect and further trust of our customers and suppliers.

If you are questioned by someone outside the Company or your department and you are concerned about the appropriateness of giving them certain information, you are not required to answer. Instead, as politely as possible, refer the request to your immediate supervisor, Chief Financial Officer, or Human Resources.

No one is permitted to remove or make copies of any FreshDirect records, reports or documents without the express written consent of the Chief Financial Officer of FreshDirect. No one is permitted to take photographs or make other electronic recordings of any kind while on Company premises. Dissemination, posting, or other disclosure of confidential information could lead to termination, as well as other possible legal action.
