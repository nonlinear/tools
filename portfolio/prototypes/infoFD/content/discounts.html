Did you know as an active FreshDirect employee you are eligible for a 20% discount on products from FreshDirect?

To start receiving the discount:

  1. Create an account at FreshDirect.com.
  2. Send us an email at HR@freshdirect.com with the following information: email address used to create FD account, Name and Employee ID number.
  3. Your discount will become effective about two weeks from the date you send the email.
  4. We will send you a confirmation email once your account is updated.
