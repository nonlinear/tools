  • Compliance: With this Code

In no instance may employees use FreshDirect facilities for personal use without the prior approval of their immediate supervisor. If granted permission to use FreshDirect facilities for personal purposes, you understand and agree that FreshDirect is not liable for personal injury incurred during the use of company facilities for personal projects. As a FreshDirect employee, you accept full responsibility for any and all liabilities for injuries or losses which occur, or for the malfunction of equipment. You are responsible for returning the equipment or tools in good condition, and you agree that you are required to pay for any damages that occur while using the equipment or tools for personal projects.
