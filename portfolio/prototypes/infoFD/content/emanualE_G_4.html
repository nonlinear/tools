Any FreshDirect property issued to you, such as product samples, computer equipment, keys, parking passes, tools, or company credit card must be returned to FreshDirect at the time of your termination. You will be responsible for any lost or damaged items. The value of any property issued and not returned may be deducted from your paycheck, and you may be required to sign a wage deduction authorization form for this purpose.
