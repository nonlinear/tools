Kronos Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I submit a request for Kronos access?

Send an email to HR-Kronos@freshdirect.com

Q: How do I reset my Kronos account?

Contact your Kronos administrator. Send an email to HR-Kronos@freshdirect.com

Q: What should I do if my clock in Kronos is not working?

Send an email to HR-Kronos@freshdirect.com

Q: Can I have my Kronos username and password sent to my outlook account? How?

Forgot Your Password Video

Q: Can an employee's hours be adjusted for a previous pay period?

Adjustments cannot be made to an employee's timecard once it is approved by a manager.

Q: Where can I find the link to Kronos Timekeeper & Self Service?

http://hr.freshdirect.com/selfservice/

Q: How can I avoid constantly being locked out of Kronos Timekeeper?

If you forget your password, do not attempt to log-in. For security reasons, Kronos will lock you out of the system after 3 attempts.

Q: What should I do if my employee is having difficulty punching-in their time?

Record the error message displayed on the screen and forward via email to HR-Kronos@freshdirect.com.

Q: Is there a Kronos user guide for managers that I can refer to?

Yes. The Kronos User Guide is available with step-by-step directions. Please click here!

Q: Is a there a Kronos hotline that can assist with troubleshooting?
