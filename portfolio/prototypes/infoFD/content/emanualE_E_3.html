All holidays will be observed on the day designated by FreshDirect. A list setting forth the dates on which FreshDirect holidays will be observed will be distributed at the start of each calendar year.

In order to qualify for holiday pay, you must work the scheduled workday immediately before and after the holiday or if you are scheduled to work on the holiday, you must work that day to qualify for holiday pay. Only pre-arranged excused absences will be considered exceptions to this policy.

If a FreshDirect-observed holiday occurs during your scheduled vacation, you will receive holiday pay for that day and you will not be charged with PTO hours (as defined below) for that day.

If you are a full-time “non-exempt” employee and work on a FreshDirect-observed holiday, you will receive your regular holiday pay plus your straight-time hourly pay (or overtime hourly pay if applicable) for any additional hours worked on the holiday.

If you are exempt and must work a FreshDirect-observed holiday, you will be granted an additional paid day off. (The terms “exempt” and “non-exempt” are defined earlier in the Employee Manual.)

You are not eligible to receive holiday pay when you are on an unpaid leave of absence.
