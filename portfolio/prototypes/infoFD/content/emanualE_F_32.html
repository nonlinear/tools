New Employees

All employees must have a FreshDirect corporate ID processed at the time they report to Human Resources and fill out the employment paperwork. The badge will be issued at no cost to new personnel. Badges must be worn at all times; in the Plant and among Transportation employees, badges must be worn in the arm bands provided. Note: The name used on the I.D. badge will be the Legal Name of the individual requiring the badge, NO NICKNAMES WILL BE USED. Employees who lose or misplace their IDs need to replace their IDs immediately or they will not have access to the facility and could be subject to disciplinary action.

Lost or Stolen Badge Replacement

  1. Any employee who has lost his or her I.D. Badge must go to the Human Resource office for a replacement; Human Resources will verify that the employee is in fact still employed and the individual will be required to pay a $5.00 lost badge fee; an armband replacement also costs $5
  2. The lost badge fee will be refunded to the individual if he or she finds and returns the lost badge to the Human Resource office within 10 business days from the date of replacement; all returned lost badges are destroyed

Damaged Badge Replacement

Any employee who brings a damaged badge to the Human Resource office will have the badge replaced at no charge so long as the damage was not caused by employee vandalism or gross negligence.

Change In Name Or Department

Any employee needing a new badge to reflect a change in Name or Department must see Human Resources. There is no charge for this badge reissue. The name used on the I.D. badge will be the Legal Name of the individual requiring the badge, NO NICKNAMES WILL BE USED.

Non-photo Identification Badges

FreshDirect will not issue Non-photo Identification Badges of any type, excluding cases of Religious Belief that restrict having an individual's picture taken
