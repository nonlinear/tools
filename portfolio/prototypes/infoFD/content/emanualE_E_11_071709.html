Occasionally, for medical, personal, or other reasons, you may need to request to be temporarily released from the duties of your job with FreshDirect. It is the policy of FreshDirect to allow its eligible employees to apply for and be considered for certain specific leaves of absence.

Absent specific approval from your supervisor for special circumstances, time off for any reason during a working day will count first against your accrued PTO in daily increments. Once you have used all of your accrued PTO, any time off will be without pay unless other treatment is specifically approved in writing by the Human Resources Department.

Failure to return to work as scheduled from an approved leave of absence or to inform the Human Resources Department of an acceptable reason for not returning as scheduled will be considered a voluntary resignation of employment.

All requests for leaves of absence shall be submitted in writing to your immediate supervisor. Each request shall provide sufficient detail such as the reason for the leave, the expected duration of the leave, and the relationship of family members, if applicable.

There are several types of unpaid leaves for which you may be eligible.
