This Employee Manual has been prepared to acquaint you with the personnel policies and benefit programs of FreshDirect. Familiarity with the contents of this manual will be helpful in many matters that relate to your employment.

The information in this manual has deliberately been presented in an informal manner and, as such is only a summary of the Company’s policies and benefits. If you have questions or wish more detailed information, you should consult with your manager or the Human Resources Department.

Separate booklets describe benefits summarized only briefly here. Of course, the actual provisions of each benefit plan or contract will govern if there is any inconsistency between this manual and the Company’s formal plans or contracts.

Please note that certain aspects of this Employee Manual may be inconsistent with the terms of a collective bargaining agreement in effect between the Company and certain unionized employees. Where possible, the terms of this Employee Manual and such an agreement should be harmonized, but where they are in conflict, members of the relevant bargaining unit will be subject to the terms of the collective bargaining agreement over this Employee Manual.

This Employee Manual is also available in Spanish. The text of the English language manual will govern to the extent there is any discrepancy between the different language versions.

Finally, this manual reflects personnel policies and employee benefits in effect at the time of the general distribution of this manual. As in any progressive organization, changes may be made from time-to-time in the policies and benefits reflected in this manual. The most current version of the manual will be provided online. Further, the Company reserves the right to modify or discontinue any of the policies or benefits summarized in this manual with or without prior notice to the employees. Also, in the event any policy in this manual is found to be inconsistent with federal, state, or local law, that policy shall be deemed modified to comply with that law. This manual replaces and supersedes all previous employment policies and practices, written and unwritten, and all prior manuals.
