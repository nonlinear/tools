  • Violence in the Workplace Policy
  • Code of Ethical Behavior
  • Compliance: With this Code

FreshDirect has adopted a “zero tolerance” policy prohibiting workplace violence. Consistent with this policy, acts or threats of physical violence, including intimidation, harassment, and/or coercion, which involve or affect FreshDirect, any FreshDirect employee, or which occur on FreshDirect property will not be tolerated and will result in discipline up to and including termination.

Such acts or threats of violence whether made directly or indirectly, by words, gestures, or symbols, infringe upon the Company’s right or obligation to provide a safe workplace for its employees. This includes (1) all threats or acts of violence occurring on FreshDirect's premises, regardless of the relationship between FreshDirect and the parties involved, and (2) all threats or acts of violence occurring off FreshDirect's premises involving someone who is acting in the capacity of a representative of FreshDirect.

Specific examples of conduct that may be considered threats or acts of violence include, but are not limited to, the following (whether initiated by the person or pursued in so-called self defense):

  1. Hitting, shoving or otherwise causing physical injury or harm to another person (please note that it will not be a defense to say that the other person hit or shoved first)
  2. Threatening an individual or his/her family, friends, associates, or property with harm
  3. Intentional destruction or threatening to destruct FreshDirect's property or the property of another employee
  4. Making harassing or threatening phone calls
  5. Harassing surveillance or stalking (following or watching someone)
  6. Unauthorized possession or inappropriate use of firearms or weapons
  7. Aggressive or hostile behavior that creates a reasonable fear of injury to another person or subjects another individual to emotional distress

FreshDirect’s prohibition against threats and acts of violence applies to all persons involved in FreshDirect's operation, including but not limited to personnel, contract, and temporary workers and anyone else on FreshDirect property.

An employee who believes that he or she has been the target of violence or threats of violence or intimidation, or has witnessed or otherwise learned of violent conduct by another employee in the capacity described above, should contact Human Resources or the Company’s security manager immediately. Employees bringing a complaint or assisting in the investigation of such a complaint will not be adversely affected in terms and conditions of employment, nor discriminated against or discharged because of the complaint.

Threats, threatening or intimidating conduct, or any other acts of aggression or violence in the workplace will not be tolerated. Any employee determined to have committed such acts will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination and/or legal action as appropriate. Non-employees engaged in violent acts on the employer’s premises or against FreshDirect’s employees will be reported to the proper authorities for prosecution.
