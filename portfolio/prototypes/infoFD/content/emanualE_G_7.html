FreshDirect does not respond to oral requests for references. In the event your employment with FreshDirect is terminated, either voluntarily or involuntarily, the Human Resources Department may be able to provide a reference to potential employers only if you have completed and signed a release form. Upon receipt of this form, the only information released will be dates of employment and your final title with the Company.

As an employee of FreshDirect, please forward the information request to the Human Resources Department.
