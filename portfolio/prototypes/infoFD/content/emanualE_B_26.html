For your convenience and comfort, FreshDirect may provide a meal room. This area is for everyone's use. It is your responsibility to do your share in keeping this facility clean and sanitary.

Please remember to use containers with reliable seals and label your food with your name and date. If there is a refrigerator available, it will be cleaned out regularly, so avoid leaving any food you wish to keep.
