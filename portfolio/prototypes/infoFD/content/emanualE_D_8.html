  • Social Security
The United States Government operates a system of mandated insurance known as Social Security. As a wage earner, you are required by law to contribute a set amount of your weekly wages to the trust fund from which benefits are paid. As your employer, FreshDirect is required to deduct this amount from each paycheck you receive. In addition, FreshDirect matches your contribution dollar for dollar, thereby paying one-half of the cost of your Social Security benefits.
