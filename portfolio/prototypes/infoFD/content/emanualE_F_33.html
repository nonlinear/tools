FreshDirect is dedicated to providing a healthy, comfortable, productive work environment for our employees. This goal can only be achieved through ongoing efforts to protect non-smokers and by helping employees adjust to restrictions on smoking. Therefore, smoking is prohibited throughout the Company facilities and in Company vehicles.
