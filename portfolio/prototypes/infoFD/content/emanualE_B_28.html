In order to apply for an internal posted position, the applicant must be a regular full-time employee of FreshDirect with acceptable performance (no discipline within the past 6 months; not on a corrective action plan)

  • Applicants must meet most experience and educational requirements as outlined in the job positing
  • Qualified temporary workers may apply for posted positions providing regular FreshDirect employees are considered first; temporary workers will be given the same opportunity as would be afforded any other external candidate
  • Meeting minimum requirements does not necessarily guarantee an interview
  • Newly hired FreshDirect employees will be eligible to take advantage of the posting process after completion of six (6) months of continuous service
  • Employees who have transferred to a new position or department will be eligible for the posting process after twelve (12) months; the Company reserves the right to grant individual exceptions based on business needs

Management-driven transfers due to Operational needs do not need to follow these procedures. Human Resource must be notified and pre-approve all Internal Transfers.
