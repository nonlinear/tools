Employees whose work requires operation of a motor vehicle must present and maintain a valid driver's license and a driving record acceptable to our insurer. All employees in such positions must consent to the Company obtaining a copy of his/her driving license abstract (motor vehicle report, or MVR) at the time of hire and upon request by management. Each employee is required to immediately inform the Company of any changes in his/her driving record. Violations of this policy may result in immediate termination. An employee who operates a vehicle will either be terminated from employment or moved into a non-driving position (with a likely commensurate drop in pay rate), if available, upon losing his/her driver’s license or being deemed uninsurable by the Company’s insurance carrier.
