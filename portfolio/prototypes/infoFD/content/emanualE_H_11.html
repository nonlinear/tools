  • Compliance: With this Code

FreshDirect recognizes the sensitive nature of having supervisors and subordinates or co-workers become personally or romantically involved at work. In order to avoid favoritism or the perception of favoritism, FreshDirect does not permit management employees to date or otherwise become romantically involved with employees within the management employee’s area of control or influence. In the event that such a relationship does exist, the management employee must notify Human Resources and one of the persons will be asked to transfer or resign or one person will be transferred or terminated. Failure to notify Human Resources of such a relationship may be grounds for termination. A manager who solicits an employee in his or her area will presumptively be in violation of the Company’s harassment policy and will be subject to discipline up to and including termination.
