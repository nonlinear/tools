If you are a full-time employee, and you have completed your Introductory Period, you will be entitled to enjoy all of the benefits described in this Employee Manual as soon as you meet the eligibility requirements for each particular benefit (for example, our health benefits and retirement savings plan are available only after 6 months of employment). Coverages are available to you and your dependents as defined in the benefit summary plan descriptions. No benefits are available to you during your Introductory Period, except as otherwise provided by law.

If you are a part-time employee, you will enjoy only those benefits specifically required by law, provided that you meet the minimum requirements set forth by law and in the benefit plan(s).

Temporary employees are not eligible for benefits.
