Workforce Timekeeper is a tool to effectively and efficiently manage your employees timecards and schedules. Use Workforce Timekeeper tools to perform daily and periodic tasks that manage employee time and labor data for payroll processing. Using Timekeeper, you can monitor timecards exceptions and resolve differences between schedule and actual time recorded on timecards, add and modify employee schedules, utilizing genies, review and approve timecards, make corrections to current pay period, and add and maintain employee data. Also query management reports in Timekeeper for department and operational needs.
