Employee Relations

The primary purpose of Employee Relations is to protect the interests, rights, and privileges of exempt and non-exempt staff in all levels of the company. This team is designated by Human Resources to function as an impartial and neutral resource to assist all employees. Employee Relations provides information relating to company policies and procedures and facilitates the resolution of problems and complaints through informal investigation and mediation. The rights and interests of all parties to disputes are considered, with the aim to achieving fair outcomes.
