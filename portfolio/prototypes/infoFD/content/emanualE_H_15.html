  • Compliance: With this Code

If you are authorized to operate a FreshDirect vehicle in the course of your assigned work, or if you operate your own vehicle in performing your job, you must adhere to the following rules:

  1. You must be licensed in your state of residence
  2. You must maintain weekly mileage reports
  3. Company-owned vehicles must be maintained according to all the manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedules to maintain valid warranties, and the manufacturer's recommended oil change schedule
  4. FreshDirect provides insurance on company vehicles, however, you will be considered completely responsible for the deductible portion of the insurance, for any accidents, fines, moving or parking violations incurred while you are operating the vehicle (see Transportation Department rules for a list of parking fines that Company will cover if incurred while an employee is making a delivery in accordance with Company guidelines)
  5. Persons not authorized or employed by FreshDirect cannot operate or ride in a company vehicle
