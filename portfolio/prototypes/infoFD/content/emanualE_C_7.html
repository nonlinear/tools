Occasionally, due to production needs or unforeseeable emergencies, your department may ask you to return to work after you have left the premises for the day, or you may even be asked to work on a day other than your regularly scheduled workdays. If this occurs, FreshDirect expects you to comply with the requests of your immediate supervisor.

Non-exempt employees who are required to return to work after they have left the Company’s premises for the day will receive a minimum of four (4) hours’ pay for such recall work. Non-exempt employees who are asked to work on other than regularly scheduled days will be paid their straight time hourly wages for such work; however, they will be paid at a rate of time and one-half for each hour that they work exceeding forty (40) hours in that particular workweek.
