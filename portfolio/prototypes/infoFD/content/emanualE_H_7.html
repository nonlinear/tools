  • Compliance: With this Code

FreshDirect has established a pleasant and relaxed atmosphere in which to work. While we must still maintain a business-like environment, we allow casual business attire during the workday. (For example: ties need not be worn). Personal hygiene, cleanliness and a neat, professional appearance are essential to our business. Work clothes are to be kept neat, clean, and in good repair. A positive, professional image is one of the most important elements contributing to the success of FreshDirect.

Everyone should keep in mind that while we enjoy casual dress, we work in a professional environment, and extreme styles and sloppy appearance are to be avoided. Please note the following guidelines in determining what appropriate business casual attire is. Any of the following are unacceptable:

  • Tattered/ripped/worn-out jeans and/or clothes
  • Tank tops, halter tops, or revealing tops
  • Shirts with large lettering, advertising, or messages
  • Warm-up or sweat suits, spandex, athletic, or beach apparel
  • Strapless or revealing “sundresses”
  • Hooded sweatshirts
  • Visible undergarments
  • Flip-flops, sandals, open-toe or open-heel shoes
  • Shorts
  • Provocative clothing

This list is not intended to be all-inclusive and serves only as a guideline. Please note that if you are not dressed appropriately, you may be requested to return home to change. If you are uncertain whether your attire is appropriate, choose to wear something else.

There may be occasions you will be asked to dress in business attire for specially announced guests, customers and/or visitors.

It will be up to all of us, as a team, to make our business-casual atmosphere a success. Should you have any questions on this matter, please consult the Manager of Human Resources.
