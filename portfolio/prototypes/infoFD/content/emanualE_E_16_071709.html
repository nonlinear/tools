  • Insurance Premium Payment During Leaves of Absence
FreshDirect will continue to pay its share of insurance premiums for employee coverage and dependent coverage (if you have elected for the payment of dependent coverage), for the maximum period of any approved FMLA or summer travel leave. While you are on any other type of unpaid leave of absence from FreshDirect, you will be responsible for paying the total premiums for your coverage and that of your dependents. Failure to do so may result in loss of coverage and possible refusal by the insurance carrier to allow your coverage to be reinstated. Please consult with the controller or the Human Resources Department to set up a payment schedule. The Company may as a convenience to employees advance payment for benefits during Summer Travel leave and deduct payment once employees return.
