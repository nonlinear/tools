In special circumstances, FreshDirect may grant you leave for a personal reason. You should request an unpaid personal leave of absence from your immediate supervisor. A personal leave of absence must not interfere with the operations of your department or FreshDirect. Your immediate supervisor will submit your request to the Human Resources Department for final approval.

Other than a summer travel leave (see below), a personal leave of absence may be granted for up to twenty (20) days during a calendar year. You will be required to use all accrued PTO time during your personal leave. If your leave is extended for more than twenty (20) days, PTO and other benefits will no longer continue to accrue. Failure to return from a leave at the time agreed will result in termination of employment. Employees will be permitted to take one (1) personal leave of absence up to the twenty (20) day maximum once every three years of employment.
