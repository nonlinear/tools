Mission Statement

The Human Resources Department at FreshDirect recognizes that the Company’s most valuable assets are its employees, and we are committed to providing high quality HR services to attract, develop, motivate and retain a diverse workforce. We look to weave the Company’s core values – Quality, Respect, Teamwork, Continuous Improvement, and Accountability – into our process and practices for recruiting, employee relations, learning & development, evaluation & compensation, and benefits.
