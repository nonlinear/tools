Thank you for joining FreshDirect! We look forward to you making a great contribution to the Company and hope that you will find your employment at FreshDirect a rewarding experience. We look forward to the opportunity to work together to create a more successful Company. We also want you to feel that your employment with FreshDirect will be a great experience for you.

You have joined an organization that has established an outstanding reputation for quality. Credit for this goes to everyone in the organization. We hope you too will find satisfaction and take pride in your work here. As a member of FreshDirect's team, you will be invited to contribute your talents and energies to further improve the environment and quality of the Company.

At FreshDirect, our goal is to provide the finest quality food products to our customers and to do this more timely, efficiently, and economically than our competitors. By satisfying the needs of our customers and exceeding their expectations, we hope that they will continue to do business with us and our Company will continue to grow.

You are a valued member of our team, and once again it is our pleasure to welcome you to FreshDirect.

The FreshDirect Management Team
