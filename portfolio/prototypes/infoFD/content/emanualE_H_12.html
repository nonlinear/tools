  • Compliance: With this Code

In order to maintain a proper business environment and prevent interference with work and inconvenience to employees and clients, the following rules will apply to solicitation for any cause and distribution of literature of any kind on Company property:

Non-employees may not solicit employees or distribute literature on Company property for any purpose, at any time. Employees may not solicit other employees or be solicited, or distribute literature, for any reason during working time. Moreover, the distribution of literature is prohibited by employees in work areas at any time.

  1. Solicitation includes, but is not limited to requests for support or money for any purpose (e.g., Girl Scout cookies, raffle tickets, religious organizations)
  2. Distribution includes, but is not limited to flyers, leaflets, advertisements or cards for any purpose (e.g., personal items, automobiles, political causes, community organizations)
  3. Working time refers to both the time the person doing the soliciting and the time of the person(s) being solicited are scheduled to work; it does not include before or after work, or during meal or rest breaks
  4. Work areas include all areas in which any employee performs work; work areas do not include the employee lunchrooms, restrooms, and parking lots

The Company will strictly enforce these rules. Any violation of this policy should be immediately brought to the attention of The Human Resources Department. Employees violating the foregoing rules will be subject to appropriate disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
