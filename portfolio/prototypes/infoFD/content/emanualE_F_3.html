At the time you are hired and periodically thereafter, you will be advised about the proper entrances and exits for our employees, as well as unauthorized areas. Our insurance company prohibits unescorted or unauthorized visitors in our facilities. If you are expecting visitors, such as clients, customers or friends, please notify your immediate supervisor. In general, friends and family members should not be invited and the Company will generally not permit these individuals into FreshDirect’s facilities. Permitting access to Company facilities to someone not authorized to be on premises is a serious matter. You are expected to abide by these rules at all times, and failure to do so will lead to disciplinary action. Also, if you see a person who appears to be a visitor who is not accompanied by security or a member of management, please contact your supervisor, security, or Human Resources immediately.

In general, except for meal breaks or where demands of an employee’s job require otherwise, employees must stay in the Company’s facilities once they arrive for work until they leave at the end of their shifts. Employees may not go to personal vehicles during their work shift. Any employee using an emergency exit or other exit for which he or she is not authorized – either for access or egress – will be subject to disciplinary action.
