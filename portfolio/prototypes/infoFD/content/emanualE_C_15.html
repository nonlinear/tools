By law, the Company is obligated to keep accurate records of the time worked by employees. An accurate record of employee work hours is also necessary for the preparation of employee paychecks. This is done by electronic time punch-in and punch-out.

You are responsible for personally and accurately recording your time and approving your timecard on a weekly basis. Non-exempt employees are required to record their time when they report to work (no earlier than the scheduled start time) and at the end of the day as scheduled. Employees must also report their time at the beginning of their meal period and at the end of the meal period. It is your responsibility to record your time on a daily basis accurately and honestly by punching in and out of our time keeping system.

No one may record hours worked on your behalf. Tampering with another's time recording is cause for disciplinary action, up to and including termination, of both employees. In the event of an error in recording your time, please report the matter to your immediate supervisor as soon as you become aware of the error. You may not begin work nor may you leave for the day without reporting a failed attempt to punch in or out.
