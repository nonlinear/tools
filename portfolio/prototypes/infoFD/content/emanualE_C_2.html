FreshDirect applies the same principles of fairness to all employees, regardless of their position in the Company and without discrimination. Several factors may influence your rate of pay. Some of the items FreshDirect considers are: the nature and scope of your job, what other employers pay their employees for comparable jobs (external equity), what FreshDirect pays their employees in comparable positions (internal equity), and individual as well as FreshDirect performance.
