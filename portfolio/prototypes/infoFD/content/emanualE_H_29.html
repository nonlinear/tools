I have received instructions for accessing the current version of the FreshDirect Employee Manual online. I agree to read and abide fully with all of the rules, policies, terms and conditions contained in it as a condition of my employment and/or continued employment. If I have any questions regarding any of the Manual’s provisions, I will consult with my manager or Human Resources.

I understand that the Employee Manual is intended to serve as a guide for employees, and that the rules, policies, and benefits contained in the Manual may be changed, amended or modified by FreshDirect at any time without notice. I am responsible for knowing the contents of this Manual and for asking for an explanation of anything that is not clear to me, that this Manual only summarizes major personnel policies, that these policies are subject to change without notice, and that this Manual replaces any and all earlier policies, communications and/or manuals which FreshDirect may have issued in the past.

Also, as an at-will employee, the Company can terminate my employment, at any time, for any reason, and with or without notice or procedural requirements. Nothing in the Manual alters my at-will status, or in any way creates an expressed or implied contract of employment, and I understand that no promises to the contrary are binding on FreshDirect unless in writing and signed by the Chief Executive Officer of the Company.
