Internal Application Now Online!

Current FreshDirect employees are now able to review and apply for positions online via the internal opportunities page.  Employees are able to complete an internal transfer application via the internet and will receive email responses or can login to their profile to view their status.  Please remember: to qualify for an internal opportunity you must be in your current position at least 6 months!

FreshDirect Employee Food Benefit Program

Did you know that FreshDirect has a monthly employee food program?  This program allows employees to purchase 1-2 Meat or Seafood items at a significant discount.  The cost is automatically deducted from the employees pay check. No need for cash, debit or credit!  This program is available to all FreshDirect employees.

Employee Appreciation BBQ’s

Annually we show our employees appreciation for their hard work with Sidewalk Summer BBQ’s on the 2nd Tuesday of each month from April – October (except July due to the company picnic).  BBQ’s are from the early morning  to mid-afternoon to allow the majority of our employees to participate.  Join us for Food, Drinks and Sweets!
