Change how the system will prioritize processing your batch(es) by changing the priority queue and/or position in the queue.

 
Three queues prioritize how quickly content will move through the system. Choose a queue for your batch(es) or select Keep Current to leave your batch(es) in their existing queue.
Within each priority queue, the system works on batches in first in, first out order by default. Batches at the front of the High priority queue will be processed first; batches at the back of the Low priority queue will be processed last. You can override the default by moving your batch(es) to the front or back of the line.
